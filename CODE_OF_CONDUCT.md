# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone involved in the CampusConnect community, whether it's a user, contributor, or maintainer, will be treated with respect and dignity.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment:
- Being respectful and inclusive of different perspectives
- Constructive communication and criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Being patient and kind

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech
- Trolling or insulting comments
- Sharing others' private information without permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Community leaders are responsible for enforcing this code of conduct. Those who do not follow the code of conduct may be removed from participating in the community.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by:
- Opening a private issue
- Emailing: campusconnect@example.com

Thank you for helping make CampusConnect a welcoming community!
